CPL Check Ride Preparation Checklist

Physical Preparation

- Sleep well the night before your check ride.
- Drink enough water before your flight.

Mental Preparation

- Know your route and contingency plans.
- Practice them mentally.

Aircraft Preparation

- Ensure aircraft is ready for flight.
- Ensure you have enough for the flight.

Day-Of Preparation

- Give yourself time to relax before the ride.
- Understand how it may affect your flight.

During Check Ride

- Keep the examiner informed of your actions.
- Manage your anxiety.

Landing & Finishing

- Follow all procedures.
- Regardless of the outcome.
