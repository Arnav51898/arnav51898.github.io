About Me

From watching airplanes soar over my childhood home in Delhi to earning my wings in the skies of Florida, aviation has always captivated me. As a kid, the sight of aircraft crossing overhead sparked an endless curiosity—I was determined to understand how they defied gravity and to one day command the cockpit myself.

That childhood fascination evolved into a dedicated pursuit, leading me to train rigorously and earn my FAA Commercial Pilot License with Instrument Rating.

I completed my training at International Aero Academy, a Part 141 FAA-certified flight school in Lakeland, Florida. Every hour in the air has taught me the non-negotiable importance of precision, preparation, and accountability—no room for error when lives depend on sound decisions.

My Vision: To join a commercial airline where the cockpit view becomes my everyday office—commanding larger aircraft, ensuring safe operations for passengers and crew, and continuing to grow in this dynamic industry.
